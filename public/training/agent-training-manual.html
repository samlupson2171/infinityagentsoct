1. Introduction

Welcome to the Infinity Weekends Agent Portal — your dedicated platform for submitting client enquiries, receiving tailored quotes, and managing bookings for stag, hen, and group travel packages.

What You Can Do

  • Submit detailed enquiries for your clients' trips
  • Receive and review personalised quotes from the Infinity Weekends team
  • Accept quotes and convert them into confirmed bookings
  • Browse pre-built Super Packages with transparent pricing
  • Use the price calculator to get instant pricing estimates
  • Track all your enquiries, quotes, and bookings in one place
Note: You must have a valid ABTA or PTS number to register and use this platform. Only approved travel agencies can access the Agent Portal.

3. Logging In

1 Go to /auth/login
2 Enter your registered email address
3 Enter your password
4 Click Sign In

You will be redirected to your Agent Dashboard upon successful login.

Tip: If you've forgotten your password, use the password reset link on the login page. A reset email will be sent to your registered address.

6. Viewing & Managing Quotes

Once the Infinity Weekends team processes your enquiry, you'll receive a quote.

How Quotes Work

  1. You submit an enquiry
  2. The team reviews it and creates a tailored quote
  3. You receive an email notification when your quote is ready
  4. The quote appears in your dashboard under "My Quotes"
  5. Click on any quote to view full details

Quote Detail Page

Each quote includes:

  • Quote Reference — Unique identifier for the quote
  • Trip Overview — Destination, hotel name, arrival/departure dates
  • Trip Statistics — Number of guests, nights, and rooms
  • Pricing — Total price and per-person breakdown
  • What's Included — List of inclusions (transfers, accommodation, etc.)
  • Events & Activities — Scheduled activities with individual pricing
  • Package Details — If linked to a Super Package

Quote Statuses

StatusMeaning
DraftQuote is being prepared (not yet visible to you)
SentQuote has been sent and is ready for your review
UpdatedQuote has been revised — review the latest version
AcceptedYou have accepted this quote

7. Accepting Quotes & Bookings

Accepting a Quote

1 Navigate to the quote detail page
2 Review all details carefully (pricing, inclusions, dates)
3 Click the Accept Quote button
4 Confirm your acceptance
5 The quote converts to a booking
Note: Once accepted, the Infinity Weekends team will confirm your booking shortly. You'll see the booking status change from "Awaiting Confirmation" to "Confirmed".

Managing Bookings

All accepted quotes appear in your My Bookings section. Each booking shows:

  • Booking reference and title
  • Destination and hotel
  • Number of guests and nights
  • Arrival date
  • Total price
  • Status (Confirmed or Awaiting Confirmation)

Click on any booking to view full details or download itineraries.

8. Browsing Super Packages

Super Packages are pre-built holiday packages with transparent, tiered pricing. They're a great way to quickly find options for your clients.

Accessing Super Packages

  • From your dashboard, click Browse all packages →
  • Or navigate directly to /dashboard/packages

Package Information

Each Super Package includes:

  • Package Name — Descriptive title
  • Destination & Resort — Where the package is based
  • Pricing Matrix — Prices vary by:
    • Travel period (month/season)
    • Group size tier (e.g., 8-12, 13-20, 21+ guests)
    • Duration (2, 3, or 4 nights)
  • Inclusions — What's included (transfers, accommodation, activities)
  • Accommodation Examples — Sample hotels/apartments
  • Sales Notes — Additional selling points

Enquiring from a Package

When you find a suitable package:

1 Select your preferred options (group size, nights, period)
2 Click Create Enquiry or Enquire Now
3 The enquiry form opens pre-filled with the package details
4 Add your client's specific details and submit
Tip: Super Packages give you instant pricing visibility. Use them to provide quick estimates to clients before submitting a formal enquiry.

9. Using the Price Calculator

The Price Calculator lets you get instant pricing estimates from Super Packages without submitting an enquiry.

How to Use

1 Open a Super Package
2 Select the travel month/period
3 Choose the group size tier
4 Select the number of nights (2, 3, or 4)
5 The per-person price updates automatically

Understanding Pricing Tiers

FactorHow It Affects Price
Group SizeLarger groups typically get better per-person rates
SeasonPeak months (summer) are more expensive than off-peak
DurationMore nights = higher total but often better nightly rate
AccommodationHotels vs self-catering have different price points

11. Troubleshooting & FAQs

I can't log in

  • Ensure you're using the correct email address (the one you registered with)
  • Check that your account has been approved — you'll receive an approval email
  • If your account is pending, you'll be redirected to a "Pending Approval" page
  • Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link

My registration was rejected

  • Check the rejection email for the specific reason
  • Common reasons: invalid ABTA/PTS number, incomplete information
  • You can re-register with corrected information

I submitted an enquiry but haven't received a quote

  • Quotes are prepared manually by the team — allow 24-48 hours
  • Check your dashboard for status updates
  • Contact support if it's been more than 48 hours

The price on my quote differs from the Super Package

  • Super Package prices are indicative and based on standard configurations
  • Final quotes account for specific dates, availability, and any custom requirements
  • Events and activities add to the base package price

I want to change my enquiry after submitting

  • You cannot edit a submitted enquiry directly
  • Submit a new enquiry with the updated details
  • Add a note referencing the original enquiry

How do I update my account details?

  • Contact the Infinity Weekends team to update your registered details
  • You can change your password from the login page

12. Support & Contact
